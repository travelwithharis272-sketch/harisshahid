About this site

Salaam, I'm Haris Shahid. I run the YouTube channel Travel Tips with Haris Shahid, where I share practical guidance for Pakistanis who want to study, work, or settle abroad.

I built this website for one reason: there is too much confusing and dishonest visa information online. People are promised "guaranteed" visas, fake shortcuts, and routes that don't actually exist — and they lose money and time. My goal here is the opposite: clear, honest, up-to-date guides that tell you the real requirements, including when something is difficult or temporary.

What you'll find here

  • Step-by-step visa and residence guides for popular destinations.
  • Honest notes on which routes are realistic — and which "shortcuts" are myths.
  • Warnings about common scams targeting Pakistani applicants.
  • Practical next steps, from documents to CVs.

Important note

I am not an immigration lawyer, and nothing on this site is legal advice. Visa rules change often. Always confirm the latest requirements on the official government website of the country you're applying to before you apply or pay anyone.
