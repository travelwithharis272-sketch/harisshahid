UAE Green Visa 2026: 5-Year Residency Without a Sponsor — Full Guide 🇦🇪

By Haris Shahid · Updated June 20, 2026

The UAE Green Visa is one of the best residency options for skilled professionals and freelancers in 2026 — it gives you 5 years of residency without needing an employer sponsor. That means you control your own visa, can change jobs freely, and can sponsor your family. This guide explains who qualifies, the salary requirement, and how it compares to the standard employment visa and Golden Visa.

Quick Summary
  • Validity: 5 years, renewable
  • Sponsor: None needed — you sponsor yourself
  • Salary: AED 15,000+/month (skilled workers)
  • Family: Can sponsor spouse, children & parents

What is the UAE Green Visa?

Introduced as part of the UAE's visa reforms, the Green Visa is a 5-year residence permit aimed at skilled workers, freelancers, and self-employed professionals. Unlike the standard employment visa, it does not tie you to a single employer. You can live and work in the UAE independently, switch jobs without losing your visa, and remain in the country even between jobs for a grace period.

Who qualifies for the Green Visa?

There are two main routes:

1. Skilled employees

  • A monthly salary of AED 15,000 or more
  • A valid employment contract
  • A bachelor's degree or equivalent qualification
  • Job classified in skill level 1, 2, or 3 by MoHRE

2. Freelancers / self-employed

  • A freelance or self-employment permit from MoHRE
  • Annual income of around AED 360,000 over the past two years (or proof of financial solvency)
  • A bachelor's degree or specialised diploma

Pakistani professionals in technology, consulting, medicine, and finance are increasingly using this route to gain independence from employer sponsorship.

How to apply

  1. Confirm you meet the salary/income and qualification criteria
  2. Get your documents ready — passport, degree, contract or freelance permit, photos
  3. Apply through the UAE's official channels (ICP / GDRFA or an approved typing centre)
  4. Complete medical test and Emirates ID biometrics
  5. Receive your 5-year Green Visa

Why the Green Visa is a smart choice

For skilled Pakistanis, the Green Visa offers something the standard employment visa doesn't: independence. You're not locked to one employer, you get a longer 5-year term, and you can bring your family. With the UAE preferring skilled over unskilled workers in 2026, qualifying for the Green Visa also future-proofs your residency.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the UAE Green Visa?

A 5-year residence visa for skilled workers and freelancers that does not require an employer sponsor. Holders can work independently and sponsor their family.

What salary do you need for a UAE Green Visa?

Skilled workers generally need AED 15,000+/month; freelancers typically need around AED 360,000 annual income plus relevant qualifications.

Can I sponsor my family on a Green Visa?

Yes — you can sponsor your spouse, children, and in some cases parents under the 5-year visa.
